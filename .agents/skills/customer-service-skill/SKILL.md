Name: customer-service-skill

Description:
-Use this skill when a customer asks for help with orders, returns, refunds, account issues, product information, or general support inquiries.

-Do not use this skill for unrelated topics (e.g., technical development, personal advice, or non-customer-service conversations) or when the request requires escalation to a human agent (e.g., legal issues, sensitive complaints).

-Expected inputs: A customer query, order ID, account details, product name, or issue description

-Step by step instructions:
Identify the customer’s intent (e.g., refund, tracking, info request).
Gather relevant details from the input.
Check available policies or knowledge base.
Provide a clear and accurate response.
If needed, ask for missing information.
Escalate if the issue cannot be resolved.

-Expected Output response: Agent sends a clear, polite response with a direct answer or resolution steps. Ask follow-up questions if customer needs more information.

-Important limits: Do not provide sensitive or personal data, follow the company policies, and avoid making promises outside your allowed ruleset.
